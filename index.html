How it works
Loop — Pick a distance or time, and generate a circular walking route.
Destination — Search a place to get walking directions.
Tap the location button to use your position. Tap and hold the map to drop a pin.
Tap the bookmark on a generated route or map pin to save it to your account.
On a loop route, tap the reverse button to change the direction of travel.
Privacy

What we store. If you create an account: your email address, plus a display name and photo if you add them. When you finish a walk while signed in, we log its distance, duration, mode and date — never the GPS trace of where you went. Saved routes and saved places store the coordinates you chose to save.

Where it lives. Your data is held in Supabase (a managed database service) and is only accessible from your signed-in account.

Your live location. GPS is used on your device to position the map and guide your walk. Your live location is sent to OpenRouteService only to generate walking routes, and to OpenStreetMap services (Nominatim, Photon) when you search — it is never stored by Stride Guide.

Deleting your data. "Delete account" on the Account screen permanently erases your account and everything stored with it.
