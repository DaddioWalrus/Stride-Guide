Iconography · two bespoke sets

Replacing the emoji

Both are complete, single-weight, grid-aligned line sets covering the app's core glyphs — every one recolourable, scalable and screen-reader-labelled, unlike 🛰️🔖↺. The choice is a matter of character: friendly-rounded, or technical-cartographic. Open-licensed sets (Lucide, Feather) sit near the Survey end — faster to adopt, but less ownable.

My read: pair the icons to the palette you pick — Fieldline with Horizon (the rounding and the coral belong together), Survey with Trailhead or Nocturne (the technical edge suits the cartographic and dark-first directions). If speed matters more than ownership, Survey can be swapped for an open-licensed set with near-zero authoring — but the bespoke sets are only ~14 glyphs and give you full control.
Two picks to lock: a palette (Trailhead / Horizon ★ / Nocturne, with Nocturne's palette as the dark theme either way) and an icon set (Fieldline ★ / Survey). Once those are chosen, we move to the tier discussion.
